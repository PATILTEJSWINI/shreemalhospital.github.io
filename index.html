    THROAT

    An important passageway between the external environment and your inner body, the throat is located behind the mouth, below the nasal cavity, and above the esophagus and windpipe. It carries food to the esophagus and air to the lungs.

    NOSE

    The smell of incense and flowers at your place of worship or that delicious home cooked food is an experience like no other. The nose is the organ of smell and a main passageway for air into and out of the lungs. The nose warms, moistens, and cleans air before it enters the lungs.

    EAR

    Sounds are everywhere, and you have two cool parts on your body that let you hear them all: your ears! Your ears are in charge of collecting sounds, processing them, and sending sound signals to your brain. And that's not all — your ears also help you keep your balance.

    About Us

    The hospital is conveniently located on the New DP road next to major township named 24K , Aundh Annexe, Pune..

    SHRIMAL E.N.T Hospital is a committed & specialized ear, nose & throat centre setup by Dr. Vijay Shinde & Dr. Mrs. Vaishali V. Shinde, an E.N.T. surgeon with 17 yrs experience. Our focus is microsurgery of the ear including surgery for improvement of hearing, cochlear implants & endoscopic sinus surgery. Comprehensive management for sleep apnea & snoring, vertigo & voice disorders is available. Facilities for laser surgery are available. We have a good speech, audiology & hearing aid centre. The centre has fully equipped class 10000 modular operation theatre with state of the art equipment like laser, rigid & fibro optic endoscopes, Sailoendoscope, zeiss operating microscope, storz 3 chip cameras, xomed microdebrider, Sutter RF cautery, Coblator.

    Dr. Vijay Shinde has done MBBS DLO(ENT) EAR NOSE THROAT SURGEON from Govt. MEDICAL COLLEGE & HOSPITAL, MIRAJ , DNB from NBE ( New Delhi) and trained in ear surgery & nasal endoscopic surgery in Mumbai. He is a Senior Consultant in the Department Of ENT, Niramaya Hospital, Panal consultant at Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital. He has been in ENT practice for 20 yrs. In spite of his hectic schedule he finds time to conduct seminars & is actively involved with holding free ENT camps in collaboration with social organizations.
